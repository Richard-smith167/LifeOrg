About Us

Who we are.

Life Org was created due to my hectic lifestyle. Being in the navy, going to school, having side jobs, and having hobbies life always got away from me. I needed a way to track all of my events and obligations in one place. When I tried to find it I either had to use multiple applications, or the design of the product made the product almost unusable. So in turn I decided to create my own, and that is how Life Org was born.
